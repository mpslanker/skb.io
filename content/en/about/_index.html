A Knowledge Base of Sorts.

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}} This site was borne out of frustration. Many times I have worked on a project only to return to it later and find that I had pretty much forgotten everything about it. Additionally, I find myself wish I to not only catalog my projects but share the things I have learned along the way and give back to the community that has helped me learn and grow. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="dark" type="section" >}}
So what does SKB stand for?

SKB can mean many things. Originally, it was an inside joke between me and some colleagues at a previous company. I like to think it means "Slanker's Knowledge Base", which is what this site is intended to be. Also I like to think of it as place to consult when "shit keeps breaking" but you can feel free to attribute your own meaning to it.
